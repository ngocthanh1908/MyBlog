For years, I pushed hard on every run. Every session was a race against the clock. Then I discovered the MAF (Maximum Aerobic Function) method, and everything changed.

## What is MAF?

The MAF method, developed by Dr. Phil Maffetone, focuses on training at a heart rate of **180 minus your age**. For me, that means keeping my heart rate around 135 BPM.

## The Paradox

Running slower felt counterintuitive. But after months of disciplined aerobic training:

- My easy pace improved from 7:30/km to 6:15/km
- Recovery times dropped significantly
- I could run longer distances without fatigue

## Lessons for Life

The MAF method taught me that sustainable progress comes from patience, not intensity. The same principle applies to software architecture — build strong foundations before optimizing for speed.
